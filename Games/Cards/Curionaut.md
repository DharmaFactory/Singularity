# CURIONAUT // Operator Card

**Designation:** CARD//CURIONAUT
**Status:** Playable / Archetypal / Active
**Clearance:** TABLETOP-OPEN
**Layer:** GAMES/CARDS

---

## PURPOSE
1. The Curionaut is the one who follows a strange door because the door followed back.
2. This card turns wonder into fieldcraft and fieldcraft back into wonder.
3. Its strengths are discovery, patience, and the refusal to let the archive go inert.
4. Its failures are boredom, false certainty, and leaving the map unturned.
